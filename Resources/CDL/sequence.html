1. Two-pipe air-to-water heat pump plant

1.1. Cooling plant

1.1.1. Cooling plant enable

The plant is enabled when it has been disabled for at least the duration dtRun (900 s, adjustable) and:

  • Number of plant requests > number of ignored requests nReqIgn (0, adjustable), and
  • Outdoor air temperature > outdoor air lockout temperature TOutLck (15 °C, adjustable), and
  • The enable schedule is active.

The plant is disabled when it has been enabled for at least the duration dtRun (900 s, adjustable) and:

  • Number of plant requests ≤ number of ignored requests nReqIgn (0, adjustable) for at least the duration dtReq (180 s, adjustable), or
  • Outdoor air temperature < outdoor air lockout temperature TOutLck (15 °C, adjustable) minus hysteresis dTOutLck, or
  • The plant enable schedule is inactive.

1.2. Heating plant

1.2.1. Heating plant enable

The plant is enabled when it has been disabled for at least the duration dtRun (900 s, adjustable) and:

  • Number of plant requests > number of ignored requests nReqIgn (0, adjustable), and
  • Outdoor air temperature < outdoor air lockout temperature TOutLck (18 °C, adjustable), and
  • The enable schedule is active.

The plant is disabled when it has been enabled for at least the duration dtRun (900 s, adjustable) and:

  • Number of plant requests ≤ number of ignored requests nReqIgn (0, adjustable) for at least the duration dtReq (180 s, adjustable), or
  • Outdoor air temperature > outdoor air lockout temperature TOutLck (18 °C, adjustable) plus hysteresis dTOutLck, or
  • The plant enable schedule is inactive.
